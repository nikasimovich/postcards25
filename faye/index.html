Magic 8 Salon: SUMMERWEEN

Another edition of the experimental literary and performance salon.

By: Faye

Txt Img Multi

SEPT 5, 2025. PINE BOX ROCK SHOP, BROOKLYN, NY.

MAGIC 8 SALON IS EXPERIMENTAL. That means you don’t have to be polished, you don’t have to be experienced, and you don’t have to have a full plan.

It’s in front of an audience, but most of the crowd will be your friends. Or friends of friends. Or friends of friends of friends.

The salon, curated and hosted by best friends Phoebe and Peyton, is part talent show and part freak show. And I mean that in the best way. After all, I was one of the performers. And I may help organize future events with them, too. I'm especially excited to be a part of the salon after the energy, freedom, SKILL and fun on Sept. 5th.

As a journalist who writes about sexuality, I'd say: In a cultural landscape rife with stigma (anti-queer, anti-trans, anti-sexual expression, and pro-conformity), it's nice to have a space for all sorts of young artists. But as an amateur performer and a party girl, I'd just say it was nice to spend a Friday night screaming at the top of my lungs as my friends showed off their cool talents.
