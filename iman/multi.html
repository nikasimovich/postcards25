Haraz Coffee, Fishtown

A Yemeni coffee shop opens in Philadelphia

By Iman Sultan

Txt Img Multi

Coffee and Pastries

Croissants, slices of cake, tarts, Dubai chocolate, and savory pastries stuffed with chicken and potato beamed behind the glass on white ceramic trays. I ordered an iced white mocha latte and a slice of Pistachio milk cake. The pistachio milk cake was moist and delectable, the nutty flavor of pistachio cream shooting through my mouth and melding with the sweet, spongy texture of the cake.

"A guest is a blessing to your house. And that’s what I’m teaching my staff as well that any customer who comes in is actually your guest, so treat them with respect and love. And just take care of them."
"Traveling is all about going to a different culture, meet new people, and try their food and have an appreciation of their culture. And Yemeni coffee shop is also bringing other cultures into Philadelphia and introducing people to the food and coffee. . . it's like building bridges."
